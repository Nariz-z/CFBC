VISION & MISSION

VISION

CFBC envisions well balanced Christian leaders and workers, empowered by the Holy Spirit, actively involved in spiritual and social transformation of the nation for the Kingdom of God.

MISSION

CFBC exists to equip and develop competent Christian leaders and workers for holistic minsitry trough excellent Theological Education based on biblical pinciples to fulfill the Great Commission.







Objectives

  1. Provide Theoligical Education grounded in the authority of the Scriptures.

  2. Train and equip leaders and workers with different ministry skills and prepare them for Christian work.

  3. Create an environment that promotes biblical excellence.

  4. Disciple, nurture and empower individuals in the ways of Jesus Christ our Lord and Master Teacher resulting to life-changing experience.

  5. Develop a servant spirit with the passion of Christ and his work.

  6. Confer and send workers to preach the gospel and disciple people who will be transformed by the power of God and the Word, mature in Christ, equipped to serve the body of Christ and actively serve God.






Courses Offered

Bachelor of Theology This program is designed to quality the students to a advance one level higher than the training be previously attained and receives a degree.
Associate in Theology This program is designed for high school graduates who would like to do pastoralministry. It is a three-year course with a diploma.
Christian Worker Course This is a two-year program designed for undergraduates who would like to pastoral work with a certificate.
Certificate in Ministrial Studies This is a one-year program designed for laymen who are in church leadership. This course is offered in Bible School Extension with a certificate.
